Why Choose LVIT Technology?
Made proudly in the USA
Unlike potentiometers, which often face wear issues over time, the ILPS-19 uses contactless LVIT technology. By working on the principle of electromagnetic induction, this sensor delivers high precision and stability without the wear-and-tear issues of traditional sensors. This makes it the perfect solution for applications where accuracy and durability are essential.
